Farah El Ghemary
Lead Robotics Mentor & STEM Educator
Creator of StoriesLab

I believe the best way to understand a complex idea is to live it as a story. As a lead robotics mentor, I have spent years watching students light up the moment an abstract concept — a sensor reading, an orbital mechanic, a chemical reaction — suddenly clicks into something real and personal.

StoriesLab grew out of that conviction. Every adventure here is an attempt to compress years of classroom insight into a short interactive journey: no jargon gates, no prerequisites — just open science told through characters you care about.

My philosophy is simple: if a child can narrate a concept back to you, they own it. Stories do that better than any textbook. That's what this platform is for.
